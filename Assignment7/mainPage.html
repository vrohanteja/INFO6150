Lays snack chips are made from potatoes, salt and oil with no preservatives added to the chip. They come in a bag, they are thin and each one is of a different size and shape. Lays chips are both salty and crunchy and they do an excellent job of fulfilling the common snack craving. Lays also manufactures a line of low fat chips for those worried about gaining weight.

Pringles snack chips are made from potatoes, salt, oil, wheat starch, rice flour and a few common food chemicals. They come in a canister and each chip is the same size and shape and they are uniformly stacked up in the canister, one on top of the other. Pringles chips are not a greasy chip and they too manufacture a low fat version of their chips.
